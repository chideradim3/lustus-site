Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about investing with Lustus Trading Limited.

General Questions

What is Lustus Trading Limited?

Lustus Trading Limited is a professionally registered investment company based in the United Kingdom. We provide digital asset trading and investment management services to over 22 million active traders across 29 countries. Our platform offers transparent, consistent daily returns through professionally managed trading portfolios.

How does the investment process work?

Getting started is simple: register an account, select an investment plan that suits your goals, make a deposit, and begin earning daily returns. Your returns are credited to your account each day and you can request a withdrawal at any time — there are no lock-in periods.

How are my daily returns calculated?

Your daily return is calculated as a percentage of your deposited principal, based on the plan you selected. For example, the Dabbler Plan earns 1.2% per day on your deposit. Returns are credited daily and compound over the duration of your investment cycle.

What are digital assets and how do they generate returns?

Digital assets include cryptocurrencies and other blockchain-based financial instruments. Our professional trading team actively manages diversified portfolios across these markets — executing trades, hedging risk, and capitalising on market movements — to generate the consistent returns distributed to our investors.

Which countries does Lustus Trading operate in?

Lustus Trading currently serves investors in 29 countries worldwide. Our platform is accessible globally and supports members from across Africa, Europe, Asia, and the Americas. Contact our support team to confirm availability in your specific region.

How can I contact customer support?

Our support team is available 24/7. You can reach us by email at support@lusinesstrading.com. We aim to respond to all enquiries within a few hours. For urgent matters, please mark your message as high priority.

Deposit Issues

How do I make a deposit?

Log in to your Lustus Trading account, navigate to the Deposit section, choose your preferred investment plan, select your payment method, and follow the on-screen instructions. Once your deposit is confirmed on the network, your investment cycle begins automatically.

What is the minimum deposit amount?

The minimum deposit starts at $250 on our entry-level Dabbler Plan. Higher-tier plans have different minimum requirements — ranging up to $10,000+ for our premium offerings. Choose the plan that best fits your investment goals.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept deposits via major cryptocurrencies including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), USDT, and other leading digital assets. We do not currently accept bank transfers or credit/debit cards. Please ensure you send funds from a wallet you control.

I made a deposit but it hasn't appeared in my account. What should I do?

Deposits typically require a minimum number of blockchain confirmations before they appear. Please allow up to 30 minutes after your transaction is confirmed on-chain. If your deposit still hasn't appeared after that time, contact our support team with your transaction ID (TXID) and we will investigate promptly.

Can I add more funds to my existing investment?

Yes. You can make additional deposits at any time. Each new deposit creates a new independent investment cycle under your selected plan, earning returns from the date of that deposit. Your existing cycle continues unaffected.

Withdrawal Issues

How do I request a withdrawal?

Log in to your account and navigate to the Withdrawal section. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw and your wallet address, then confirm the request. Withdrawals are processed instantly — no delays, no waiting periods.

How long does a withdrawal take to process?

Withdrawals are processed instantly upon request. Once approved, funds are sent directly to your wallet. Network confirmation times vary by blockchain but are typically completed within minutes.

Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?

A minimum withdrawal threshold may apply depending on your plan. Please refer to your plan's terms within your account dashboard for the exact minimum. This threshold is in place to ensure network transaction fees do not exceed the withdrawal amount.

Are there any withdrawal fees?

Lustus Trading does not charge any platform withdrawal fees. However, standard blockchain network (gas) fees apply to all crypto transactions and are deducted from your withdrawal amount. These fees are set by the network, not by us.

Account Issues

How do I create an account?

Click the Sign Up button on any page of our website. Fill in your name, email address, and create a secure password. Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email to verify your account. You can then log in and make your first deposit.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password". Enter the email address associated with your account and we will send you a password reset link. If you do not receive the email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

Can I have more than one account?

No. Each person is permitted to hold only one account with Lustus Trading Limited. Creating multiple accounts violates our terms and conditions and may result in all associated accounts being suspended. If you have a legitimate need for a second account, please contact support.

How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account and go to the Account Settings section. From there you can update your personal details, email address, and notification preferences. For changes to sensitive information such as your registered name, please contact our support team for assistance.
